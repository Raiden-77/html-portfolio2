The Best Movies According to Zero.

My Top 3 Movies.

The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

A powerful story of hope, friendship and perseverance, following a man wrongly convicted of murder as he endures harsh realities of prison life.

Drive My Car (2021)

A slow-burning yet impactful Japanese drama that explores grief, human connections and the power of art over an epic 3-hour runtime.

Top Gun: Maverick (2022)

An exhilarating sequel that not only recaptures the magic of the original Top Gun but exceeds it with breathtaking action sequences and a well-crafted story
